Many people will experience muscle and joint pain which influences their quality of life. This could be because of osteoarthritis or because of a sport injury or an accident. At the Orthoclinic of Aruba we are there to help you with your problem. If you are local and are having AZV insurance, you will need a referral letter from your general practitioner. If you are here on a vacation you can walk in during opening hours or call us for an appointment, also if you’re looking for a second opinion we’re happy to help you. The Orthopaedic surgeon will hear your story, perform a physical exam and, if necessary, require additional examinations like X-rays or an ultrasound. Together with the Orthopaedic surgeon a treatment plan will be made and/or advise will be provided. Treatment options could include general recommendations, painkillers, intra-articular injections / aspiration. In case of an accident, sometimes an operation is indicated.
